Account Deletion Request - GS Fitness (GymSupport)

Last updated: July 18, 2026

If you would like to delete your GS Fitness (GymSupport) account and associated data, follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to nkg109204@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address registered to your GS Fitness account

We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm by email once completed.

What Gets Deleted

  • Account information (email, display name, profile photo)
  • Workout and app usage data (selected muscle groups/exercises, progress)
  • Subscription and reminder preferences stored in our system

What May Be Retained

  • Transaction records required for financial/tax/legal compliance may be retained for a limited period as required by law, then deleted.
  • Purchase and subscription history managed directly by Google Play is controlled by your Google Account and is not deleted by us — see Google Play Help to manage that data.

Contact

Questions about account deletion can be sent to: nkg109204@gmail.com
