Privacy Policy - GS Fitness (GymSupport)

Last updated: July 18, 2026

GS Fitness (application ID com.gymsupport.app, referred to as "GymSupport" or "we") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address, display name, profile photo — when you sign up or sign in via email or Google Sign-In (through Firebase Authentication).
  • Profile photo: if you upload a photo, it is stored using the Cloudinary service.
  • App usage data: selected muscle groups/exercises, workout progress, and in-app workout reminders.
  • Subscription information: Premium plan status (active/expired) and purchase history processed through Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store your payment card details — all payment processing is handled by Google Play.
  • Push notifications: to send workout reminders, the app requests Notification and Exact Alarm permissions on your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Camera photos/videos (except when you actively choose a photo to upload as your avatar)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate and manage your account
  • To provide and personalize workout content
  • To process and verify Premium subscription purchases
  • To send workout reminder notifications

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google) — account authentication
  • Google Play Billing — Premium subscription payment processing
  • Cloudinary — profile photo storage
  • MongoDB Atlas — application data storage

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on access-controlled servers. We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, though no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with a new update date.
